# 4-Minute Readiness Defense Video

Maximum length: 4 minutes.

This is not a tutorial recording. This is your readiness defense.

## Required Structure

### 0:00-0:30 - Decision

State:

- your assigned incident
- your readiness decision: Ship, Block, or Conditional
- the critical workflow you verified

### 0:30-1:30 - Before-Fix Evidence

Show:

- app appeared healthy
- exact workflow failure or risk
- evidence from logs/API/UI/code

### 1:30-2:30 - Root Cause and Fix

Explain:

- root cause, not just symptom
- minimal fix applied
- why you did not chase a false hypothesis

### 2:30-3:30 - After-Fix Verification

Show:

- Analyze Resume result
- score/signals
- status update
- dashboard state
- refresh/reload sanity where relevant

### 3:30-4:00 - Risk and Founder Update

State:

- remaining risk
- what you would tell the founder before the demo

## Strong Video

- shows evidence, not claims
- uses exact workflow language
- admits risk honestly
- explains why health check was insufficient

## Weak Video

- says "it works now" without showing the workflow
- reads the brief aloud
- hides remaining risk
- blames AI or the starter code
- shows only `/health` and login
